I am associate professor of Software Engineering and Technical Computing at Utrecht University of Applied Sciences (HU). I have written various articles on Computer Science & Education and many parts of *as-yet* unpublished books and stories (both science and fiction). 

Over the years I've had many ideas for papers or books, and I only just now realised they often share a common theme, which is the reason to create this blog and which is expressed in the motto ***ex principia omnia***: everything follows from the beginning.
