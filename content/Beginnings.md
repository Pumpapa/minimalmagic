[I]({{< ref "PumWalters" >}} "Pum Walters") once gave a freshman course on system engineering. [The magic of system engineering]({{< ref "TheMagicOfSystemEngineering" >}} "The magic of system engineering") is that once you understand a transistor, you can mostly understand an entire CPU. Later, I started writing a book on term rewriting with a similar foundation: *ex principia omnia*. This gave me the idea to start a blog with work and musings on that underlying theme. 

Section SE offers a freshman-level introduction to System Engineering.
